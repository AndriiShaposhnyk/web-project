Superman

A long time ago, on a distant planet called Krypton, a baby named Kal-El was born. His planet was about to be destroyed, so his parents put him in a small spaceship and sent him to Earth — hoping he would survive.

That baby landed in a small town in Kansas, where he was found and adopted by a kind farming couple, the Kents.

They named him Clark Kent and raised him like a normal boy. But Clark was not normal.As he grew older, he discovered he had amazing powers:

  • He could fly
  • He was super strong
  • He had laser vision
  • And he could hear things from miles away
When he became an adult, he moved to the city of Metropolis and became a reporter. But secretly, he used his powers to help people as Superman — the world’s first superhero.

He wore a red cape, a blue suit, and a bold "S" on his chest. People called him the Man of Steel, because nothing could hurt him. Superman always fights for truth, justice, and hope.

He protects the weak, fights evil, and reminds us that goodness is powerful — no matter where you come from.

Batman

In the heart of the dark and dangerous city called Gotham, a young boy named Bruce Wayne lived in a huge mansion with his rich parents.

One night, while walking home after a play, Bruce and his parents were attacked by a robber. The man stole from them — and then did something terrible: he killed Bruce’s parents right in front of him.

Bruce was heartbroken. He promised that one day, he would make sure no one else would suffer like he did.

As he grew up, Bruce trained his body and mind. He studied science, crime, technology, and martial arts. He became

  1. Strong
  2. Smart
  3. Fearless

But he had no powers. He was just a man. So he became something more. Something that criminals would fear. He became Batman.

Dressed like a bat, with a dark cape, sharp gadgets, and the famous Batmobile, Batman watches over Gotham at night.

He hides in the shadows, hunts criminals, and protects the city from villains like the Joker, Two-Face, and The Riddler.

By day, he is Bruce Wayne — a billionaire. By night, he is the Dark Knight — a symbol of justice, discipline, and never giving up.

Batman doesn’t have powers. He has courage, intelligence, and determination — and that’s what makes him a true hero.
